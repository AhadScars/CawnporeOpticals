Our Story

Clarity and craft from the city of Cawnpore

Since 1998

Visionaries of Kanpur

Cawnpore Opticals began as a small fitting studio on the bustling streets of old Kanpur — known historically as Cawnpore. What started with a passion for precise prescriptions has grown into a trusted name for eyeglasses, sunglasses, and complete eye care.

Today we blend traditional craftsmanship with modern materials: lightweight titanium, blue-light protection, UV400 lenses, and styles that suit every face and lifestyle. Whether you need office-ready frames or sport sunglasses, we help you see — and look — better.

Every pair is checked by trained opticians. We offer prescription fitting, free adjustments, and a straightforward warranty so you shop with confidence.
